About FFF

The Fun & Fitness Festival is UCSB Recreation's annual showcase event that takes place during UCSB's Week of Welcome in the Fall quarter of the new academic year. The festival showcases the many offerings of the Recreation Department including programs by Gaucho REC (Recreation and Exercise Classes), Rec Sports, Gaucho Pulse (Health and Fitness Services), Adventure Programs and More. Information and sign ups are available for many offerings such as Intramurals, Sports Clubs, and Athletic Training.
